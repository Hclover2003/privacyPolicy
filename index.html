PRIVACY POLICY FOR COVID19 STATS APP

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Covid19 Stats for mobile devices that was created by cloversStudio. The Application is a simple app to view the latest covid-19 / coronavirus stats for different countries/regions. The stats include the total confirmed cases, total deaths, new cases, new deaths, and a mortality rate (the total deaths/total confirmed) by country and is updated daily.
